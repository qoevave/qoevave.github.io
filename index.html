Quality of Experience Evaluation of Interactive Virtual Environments with Audiovisual Scenes
  • Inverative 6-DoF CGI scene database offering three high quality audiovisual spatial environments in Unity with acoustic geometry meshes.
  • Scene Database including 360 degree 8k video and 4th-order Ambisonics ambiX format audio content of 12 different scenes with multiple scene versions.
  • Saliency Database with 3-DoF viewing exploration for various audio scenarios and scene descriptions from verbalization tasks.
